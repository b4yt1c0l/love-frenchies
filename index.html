Frenchies: The "Borg" Community

Not a dog

Frenchies are not dogs, they act as Borg aliens in Star Trek. They are here to conquer the galaxy, and to enslave humans to get food and cuddles.

A lot of fun

Frenchies are completely "mental", you can't control them. They can decide to run around panting without any reason, or they jump up and down from your sofa trying to nibble your fingers. In any case you'll get a lot of fun with them around.
