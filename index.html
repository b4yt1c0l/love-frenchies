Frenchies: The "Borg" Community

Not a dog

Frenchies are not dogs, they act as Borg aliens in Star Trek. They are here to conquer the galaxy, and to enslave humans to get food and cuddles.

A lot of fun

Frenchies are completely "mental", you can't control them. They can decide to run around panting without any reason, or they jump up and down from your sofa trying to nibble your fingers. In any case you'll get a lot of fun with them around.

The Collective

In their collective state, the Frenchies are utterly without mercy; driven by one will alone: the will to conquer. They are beyond redemption, beyond reason.

Social Events

Frenchies are too lazy to be really interested in social events. However, they like to be all together analysing human behaviour while experimenting new mind manipulation techniques.

Clongriffin collective

One of the largest "Borg" collective is in Clongriffin. There is a weekly stand up meeting every Sunday at 9:00 AM at Father Collins Park. New members are welcome!
